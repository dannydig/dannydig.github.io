Requirements and Format

Attendance and Participation

Class lectures will start promptly at the scheduled class start time. Attendance at every class is required (for students taking the synchronous section of the class) as we will conduct individual and group classroom activities on Zoom, and students are encouraged to participate in activities and class discussions of course topics. Lectures will also be recorded and posted on the class Canvas site for later viewing. Because of the remote nature of this semester, participation will primarily be graded based on iClicker activity (for students attending synchronously) and Piazza contributions and comments (for students attending asynchronously). This will be detailed in class.

Given that there are no physical space constraints on Zoom, students who are registered in the asynchronous sections of the class and whose schedule permits them to join the class synchronously, are allowed and even encouraged to attend the synchronous offering. They will experience an increased sense of community, which is important especially during these times of social distancing.

Class materials will be made available on Canvas along with any assignments. Students are expected to keep up with the course material and assignments as they are presented. If you fall behind, have personal issues which prevent participation, or need other help, schedule an appointment with the class staff as soon as possible.

Students are expected to complete assignments on time, extensions to class assignment dates will be very rare. In some cases, project assignments will be accepted late, but the grade earned on the assignment will be reduced â€“ this allowance and reduction will be made clear for each assignment. Details of late policies will be reviewed in class as needed.

Canvas

Students must have access to and accounts for Canvas to find materials, get class notices, submit homework, and take quizzes and/or exams. I will use the announcements feature of Canvas for posting information on assignments and class changes. Please ensure you can reach the class Canvas site (https://canvas.colorado.edu/courses/70627). You should ensure that you sign up in Canvas account settings for instant notifications of news, class schedule or assignment changes, content postings, assignment and quiz due dates, and grade updates.

Piazza

For messaging and postings of discussion materials, we will be using Piazza, which is available for the web as well as iOS and Android apps. Please sign up for your Piazza connection at: piazza.com/colorado/spring2021/csci44485448. The class staff will try to answer questions posted on Piazza as quickly as possible. We will also use Piazza for posting articles and links related to class subjects.

How do I access Piazza?

If this is the first time you are using Piazza for this class, please first sign up on Piazza. For subsequent uses, you should bookmark and find our discussion forum address.

What is the CU iClicker?

iClicker is an in-class student response system designed to better engage students in the learning process and also provide instant feedback to the student. Past research has shown the clicker to be very effective at improving student/lecturer interactions, which in turn are known to improve student learning (which is what we care about) and, consequently, student grades (what you care about). For more information, please see this page.

Do I lose iClicker points if I have to miss a class due to illness, interview, emergency?

You can miss up to three classes without getting any penalty on your clicker points. That is, when we compute your final score, we will drop the three lowest clicker grades (e.g., we will not count the three times when you missed the class and got zero points). As long as you don't miss too many classes, there would not be a problem. This policy aims precisely at situations when people travel for interviews, etc., so you don't have to explicitly contact course staff for permission to miss a class.

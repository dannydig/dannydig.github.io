Communicating with the Staff

You can get faster responses if you post in Piazza. For your meetings with the staff regarding team projects, you should primarily email your assigned staff, but if some issue remains unresolved, please feel free to email Prof. Danny Dig.

If you have questions on a homework/project assignments:

  1. Read the homework itself to see if it has been updated to address your question.
  2. Check Piazza to see if your issue has been addressed there.

You will get the fastest reply by using Piazza, as any of the staff will be able to see your message quickly. Also, other students who are looking to earn extra-credit might be answering your question much faster on Piazza.

If you must email the staff, please always begin your subject line with "OOAD:". If you don't we might not see your mail.

Thank you!
